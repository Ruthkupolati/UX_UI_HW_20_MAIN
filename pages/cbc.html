PROJECT OVERVIEW


In this project, I worked in a group of 4 students, We were tasked with the responsibility of revamping CBC’s website and mobile platforms


Duration: 2-3 weeks


Methodology: We implemented the design thinking methodology to figure out a way to improve the user experience


Steps shown in this case study: Heurestic Evaluation, Usability Tests, White board brainstorming & Findings and final design shown


Role

My role in this project was User Researcher and Wireframe Designer


Tools Used

Figma, Miro, Invision, Google Tools


Project Duration

2–3 weeks


After a brief run through of the website and conducting a detailed heurestic evaluation, here’s what we figured was wrong with the website:


1. Text intensive: Although, this is a news outlet and should be text heavy, the homepage was too overwhelming.

2. Arrangement of Tiles: Not cohesive and needed to have a flow.

3. Information architecture: This was a noticeable issue as the nav bar and footer section was crowded and were very repetitive


Usability Test Findings


. Navigation Bar

. Not personalized to the user

. Location did not appear it could be personalized

. Dropdown was not as effective, the users scrolled till they found an article that was relevant to them

. Expected the radio function to be stand-alone and not in the dropdown menu

. Struggled to find location on mobil app

. Homepage was overwhelming

. Radio not visible in mobile

. Overwhelmed by content on homepage in mobile




User Persona


With our user tests, heurestic evaluation and considering the services CBC offer, we came developed a proto persona to humanize our users and help make decisions that would best serve them
